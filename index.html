Our Eternal Salvation

JESUS CHRIST is the Savior of the World.

He carried the weight of sin of the whole World. He bore man’s iniquity and stood between the wrath of GOD and guilt of man.

SALVATION means...

Deliverance | Safety | Soundness | Restoration | Healing | Substitution | Redemption | Reconciliation | Propitiation | Conviction | Repentance | Preservation | Faith | Regeneration | Sanctification | Forgiveness | Justification | Glorification We have been Redeemed and Forgiven.

Our Spirit has been Regenerated. We are Sanctified. The demands of GOD were met through Propitiation. We are Justified by Faith. We are under Righteousness that has been Imputed over us. We have been Expitiated (cancellation of everything we did, extinguish the guilt of, put an end to). We will be Glorified.
